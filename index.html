Female-led Businesses

Blaqbase

Blaqbase was founded by Benedicta Banga in 2019 as a shopping and discovery app. The app makes it easy to discover and support black owned businesses and find high quality and premium brands. Some of the brands available include beauty products such as Elenge, Flora and Curl and Hay London, and clothing and accessories including We Are Kin and Relic. The app aggregates multiple brands, which you can buy in one place using the single checkout - plus most of them deliver worldwide!

String Theory London

Welcome to String Theory London! Selling naturally hand-dyed Macrame homewares, made with 100% recycled cotton cords. Sustainability is core to String Theory London’s values, so every product is made with the environment in mind. Every cord has been hand dyed using only natural ingredients. Head over to String Theory’s Instagram and Facebook to see the dyeing process and creation of products!

Akilis Art

Akili is a freelance Illustrator and graphic designer based in London. Akilis Art create unique prints and t-shirts using bright colours and eye catching designs. They are all about caring for the planet. All prints and T shirts are sustainably produced and packaged. The prints are produced by a carbon neutral company and are delivered using recyclable envelopes, 100% recycled paper tape and biodegradable bubble wrap. You can also request your own bespoke commission too!

Box42

Box42 is a business founded with purpose by Caroline Haegman. In these changing times, Box42 aims to bring love, laughter and fun back into the home. Made for couples by a couple, their date night boxes are designed to help you and your significant other connect from the comfort of your own home. Box42 can be shipped to anywhere in the UK and contains a full date night guide, fun activities, accessories, snacks, drinks and a curated playlist!

Designed By Kate

Designed by Kate is a local business based in Walsall. Kate makes handmade earrings and rings, made with real pressed flowers.

Bennett's Bakes

Bennett’s Bakes is a homemade baking company, selling everything from terry’s chocolate orange cheesecake to mint aero cakesicles. Based in Leicester, Bennett’s Bakes offers affordable and delicious baked goods.

Panda on the Kickz

Panda on the Kickz creates personalised shoes and clothing.
